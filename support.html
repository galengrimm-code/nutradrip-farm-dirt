Getting Started

New to Soil Analysis App? Here's how to get started:

  1. Sign in with Google - Click "Sign In" on any page to connect your Google account
  2. Connect a Google Sheet - Go to Settings and enter your Sheet ID, or create a new Sheet
  3. Import your data - Use the Import page to upload soil samples, field boundaries, and yield data
  4. Explore your fields - View samples on the map, analyze trends, and correlate with yields

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Google Sheet?

Go to the Settings page and scroll to "Google Sheets Connection". Enter the Sheet ID from your Google Sheet URL (the long string of letters and numbers between /d/ and /edit). Click "Test Connection" to verify.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored in YOUR Google Sheet - you own and control it completely. The app also caches data locally in your browser for faster loading. We do not store your data on any external servers.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is protected by Google's security. We use Google's official OAuth authentication and never see your password. Since data stays in your Google account, there's no risk of breach from our end.

How do I revoke the app's access to my Google account?

Visit myaccount.google.com/permissions, find "Soil Analysis App" in the list, and click "Remove Access". Your data in your Google Sheet will remain - only the app's permission to access it is removed.

What file formats can I import?

The app supports multiple formats:

  • Soil Samples: CSV, Shapefile (.shp), GeoJSON, or ZIP containing shapefiles
  • Field Boundaries: GeoJSON, Shapefile, or ZIP
  • Yield Data: Shapefile (compatible with John Deere and Climate FieldView exports)
Why aren't my samples showing on the map?

Check these common issues:

  1. Make sure you've signed in and connected a Google Sheet
  2. Verify samples were imported successfully (check count on Import page)
  3. Check the Field and Year filters - try "All Fields" and "All Years"
  4. Zoom in - samples appear as markers at closer zoom levels
How do I delete my data?

You have several options:

  • Clear from app: Use the "Clear" buttons on the Import page
  • Delete Sheet: Delete the Google Sheet directly in Google Drive
  • Clear local cache: Clear browser localStorage in your browser settings
How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Email us at galen@galengrimm.com with a description of the issue or your feature idea. Include screenshots if possible, and let us know which browser you're using.
